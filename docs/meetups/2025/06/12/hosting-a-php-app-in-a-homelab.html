Hosting a PHP app in a Homelab

June 12th, 2025

We all love fun and exotic ways to deploy web applications, but sometimes you just want to keep things simple. You might reach for a VPS - but why not try hosting that app from your house? With Cloudflare Tunnels and a cheap mini PC you can easily and securely deploy a web application directly from your closet!
