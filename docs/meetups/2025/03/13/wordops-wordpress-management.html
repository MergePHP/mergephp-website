WordOps Wordpress Management

March 13th, 2025 Watch on YouTube

Discover the power of WordOps, a modern solution for WordPress and PHP project management, in this technical talk. We'll start by walking you through the setup process of WordOps, equipping you with the knowledge to deploy and manage WordPress sites efficiently. We will also delve into migrating existing sites to WordOps, highlighting strategies for a smooth transition with minimal downtime. We'll also explore how WordOps isn’t just limited to WordPress—it’s a robust tool that can streamline the creation and management of any PHP project. Finally, we’ll introduce WordOps Server Observability, a critical feature that provides insight into your server’s performance and health, ensuring you can monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize your environment effectively.
