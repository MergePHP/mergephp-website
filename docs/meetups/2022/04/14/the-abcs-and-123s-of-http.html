The ABCs and 123s of HTTP

April 14th, 2022 Watch on YouTube

This talk is the ABCs and 123s of HTTP. We look at the verbs, the status codes, the headers as well as things like CORS, OpenAPI, and JSON Schema

Presented by Matt Trask

He is a backend team lead at a healthcare company doing fun things with PHP. You can find him on Twitter @matthewtrask
