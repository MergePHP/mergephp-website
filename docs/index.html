Join us on May 11th, 2023 as Matthew Weier O'Phinney presents What's up with Zend, er, Laminas?

At the beginning of 2020, Zend spun off Zend Framework to the Linux Foundation under the name "The Laminas Project". When it did so, it also spun off its two subprojects, Apigility and Expressive, as Laminas API Tools and Mezzio, respectively.

What has the project been up to since then? And what are some of the things you can expect in the future? Who's running the project now?

Come join Matthew Weier O'Phinney as he tells the tale of two frameworks.
